Claude AI Kya Hai?

Claude AI ek powerful artificial intelligence assistant hai jo Anthropic company ne banaya hai. Yeh ChatGPT ke jaisa kaam karta hai lekin bahut zyada smart aur accurate hai. Claude se aap articles likh sakte hain, code bana sakte hain, sawaalon ke jawab pa sakte hain aur bahut kuch kar sakte hain — bilkul Hindi mein!

Claude AI Kaise istemal Kare?

Claude AI istemal karna Aaj bilkul Aasan ho gya . Claude.ai par jao, free account banao aur chat shuru karo. Apna sawaal Hindi mein likho — Claude Hindi mein jawab dega! No technical knowledge needed.

Claude AI Se Paise Kaise Kamaye?

Claude AI sirf ek chatbot nahi — yeh aapka earning tool hai! Yahan kuch tarike hain jisse aap Claude se paise kama sakte hain:

1. Content Writing

Claude se articles, blog posts, social media content likhwao aur clients ko becho. Fiverr par ₹500-5000 per article milta hai!

2. YouTube Scripts

Claude se video scripts likhwao aur YouTubers ko becho. Ek script ₹300-1000 me Bik Sakta Hai!

3. Email Writing

Businesses ke liye professional emails Claude se likhwao. Ek email ₹100-500 mein bik sakti hai!

4. Blog Management

Doosron ke blogs ke liye Claude se content banao aur monthly retainer lo — ₹5000-20000 mahine!

5. Social Media Manager

Claude se Instagram, Facebook posts banao aur clients ke accounts manage karo — ₹3000-10000 mahine!

Claude AI Vs ChatGPT — Kaunsa Better?

Dono achhe hain — lekin Claude zyada Gahrai se aur accurate jawab deta hai. Long articles likhne mein Claude best hai. ChatGPT coding mein thoda better hai. Dono free mein use kar sakte hain!
